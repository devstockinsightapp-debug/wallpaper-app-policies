Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: 20-02-202

Quick Summary: You can request a refund within 48 hours of purchase if you're unsatisfied with a wallpaper. After 48 hours, all sales are final.

1. General Refund Policy

We want you to be satisfied with your purchases. If you're not happy with a wallpaper, you may request a refund within the timeframe specified in this policy.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

2.1 Time Limit

Refund requests must be submitted within 48 hours of the purchase date and time.

2.2 Valid Reasons for Refund

Refunds may be granted for the following reasons:

  • You are not satisfied with the wallpaper quality
  • The wallpaper doesn't match the preview shown in the App
  • Technical issues prevented you from downloading the wallpaper
  • You accidentally purchased the wrong wallpaper
  • The wallpaper appears distorted or incorrect on your device
  • Duplicate purchase of the same wallpaper

2.3 Refunds NOT Available

Refunds will NOT be granted in the following cases:

  • Purchase was made more than 48 hours ago
  • You simply changed your mind after the 48-hour window
  • The wallpaper was successfully downloaded and used
  • You violated our Terms of Service
  • The wallpaper was removed from the App due to your Terms violation

3. How to Request a Refund

Important: All refund requests must be processed through Google Play, not directly through our App.

Method 1: Through Google Play Store

  1. Open the Google Play Store app on your device
  2. Tap the menu icon (three horizontal lines)
  3. Select "Account"
  4. Tap "Purchase history"
  5. Find the wallpaper purchase you want refunded
  6. Tap "Request a refund"
  7. Select your reason and submit

Method 2: Through Google Play Website

  1. Visit play.google.com/store/account
  2. Sign in with the Google account used for the purchase
  3. Find the purchase in your order history
  4. Click "Request a refund"
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions

Method 3: Contact Us Directly (Backup Method)

If you have trouble requesting a refund through Google Play, you can contact us at:

Email: dev.stockinsight.app@gmail.com

Include the following information:

  • Your Google Play order number
  • Date and time of purchase
  • Name of the wallpaper
  • Reason for refund request
  • Screenshot of purchase confirmation (if available)

We will process your request and respond within 2-3 business days.

4. Refund Processing

4.1 Processing Time

Approved refunds are typically processed by Google Play within:

  • Instant to 48 hours: For requests made within 2 hours of purchase
  • 3-5 business days: For requests made between 2-48 hours after purchase

4.2 Refund Method

Refunds are issued to your original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Card: Refund appears on your card statement within 5-10 business days
  • Google Play Balance: Refund credited immediately after approval
  • Other Payment Methods: Timeframe varies by provider

4.3 After Refund is Processed

Once a refund is approved:

  • You will lose access to the refunded wallpaper
  • The wallpaper will be removed from your "Purchased" list
  • You can purchase it again in the future if desired
  • Any downloaded copies on your device should be deleted

5. Google Play's Refund Policies

In addition to our policy, refund requests are subject to Google Play's policies:

  • Google offers a 2-hour automatic refund window for most purchases
  • After 2 hours, refunds are at the discretion of the developer (us) and Google
  • Google reserves the right to deny refund requests for abuse
  • Repeated refund requests may result in restrictions on your Google account

Full details: Google Play Refund Policy

6. Cancellation Policy

6.1 No Subscriptions

The App does not use subscriptions. All purchases are one-time payments for individual wallpapers. There is nothing to cancel.

6.2 Stopping Unwanted Charges

Since all purchases require explicit confirmation:

  • You cannot be charged without actively purchasing a wallpaper
  • No recurring charges will occur
  • Each purchase requires your authorization through Google Play

6.3 Preventing Accidental Purchases

To avoid accidental purchases:

  • Enable "Require authentication for purchases" in Google Play settings
  • Review the purchase confirmation before confirming
  • Use Google Play parental controls if needed

7. Duplicate Purchases

If you accidentally purchase the same wallpaper twice:

  • Contact us immediately with proof of both purchases
  • We will process a refund for the duplicate purchase
  • Duplicate purchases are eligible for refund even beyond 48 hours

8. Technical Issues

If you experience technical problems preventing download or use:

  • Contact us at dev.stockinsight.app@gmail.com with details of the issue
  • Include your device model and Android version
  • We'll try to resolve the issue first
  • If unresolvable, a full refund will be provided

9. Refund Abuse

We reserve the right to deny refunds if we detect abuse, including:

  • Excessive refund requests (requesting refunds for most purchases)
  • Pattern of purchasing, downloading, and then requesting refunds
  • Violating our Terms of Service
  • Attempting to defraud the system

Abuse may result in:

  • Denial of future refund requests
  • Suspension of App access
  • Reporting to Google Play for account action

10. Partial Refunds

We do not offer partial refunds. Each wallpaper purchase is refunded in full or not at all.

11. Regional Variations

Consumer protection laws vary by region. If your local laws provide additional refund rights beyond this policy, those rights remain in effect. This policy represents our baseline commitment but does not supersede stronger consumer protections in your jurisdiction.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy when changes are made.

13. Contact for Refund Questions

If you have questions about refunds or need assistance:

Email: dev.stockinsight.app@gmail.com
Response Time: Within 2-3 business days
Developer: VJD Insights

14. Fair Use Commitment

We are committed to fair treatment of all customers. If you have a legitimate concern about a purchase, we will work with you to find a reasonable solution, even if it falls slightly outside the strict terms of this policy.
